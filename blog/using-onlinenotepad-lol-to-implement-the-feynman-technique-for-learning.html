What is the Feynman Technique?

The Feynman Technique, named after physicist Richard Feynman, is a learning method that emphasizes understanding through teaching. The core idea is that if you can't explain a concept simply, you don't understand it well enough. This technique involves four key steps:

  1. Choose a concept you want to learn.
  2. Explain the concept in simple terms.
  3. Identify gaps in your understanding and revisit the source material.
  4. Review and simplify your explanation further.
