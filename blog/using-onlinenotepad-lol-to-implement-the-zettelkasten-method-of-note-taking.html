Understanding the Zettelkasten Method

The Zettelkasten method is a powerful note-taking system developed by German sociologist Niklas Luhmann. It focuses on creating a network of interconnected notes, allowing users to capture and organize their thoughts effectively. By utilizing this method, you can enhance your understanding, retention, and creativity in any subject area.
