What is GTD?

GTD is a time management method that encourages you to capture all tasks, ideas, and commitments in an organized way, allowing you to focus on executing tasks rather than remembering them. The five key stages of GTD are:

  1. Capture: Collect all tasks and ideas into a trusted system.
  2. Clarify: Process what these items mean and decide what to do with them.
  3. Organize: Sort tasks into appropriate categories.
  4. Reflect: Review your tasks regularly to stay on track.
  5. Engage: Take action on your organized tasks.
