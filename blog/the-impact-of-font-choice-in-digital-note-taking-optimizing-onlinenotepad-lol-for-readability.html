Additional Considerations for Digital Note-Taking

In addition to font choice, consider the following factors to enhance your digital note-taking experience:

  • Regular Reviews: Periodically revisit and refine your notes to ensure they remain organized and easy to read. This practice promotes better retention and understanding of the material.
