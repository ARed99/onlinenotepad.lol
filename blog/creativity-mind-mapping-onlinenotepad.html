What is Mind Mapping?

Mind mapping is a visual tool used to organize ideas in a non-linear way, helping individuals and teams brainstorm, structure information, and uncover new insights. By representing thoughts and ideas visually, it encourages free thinking and allows for the exploration of concepts that might not emerge through traditional note-taking.

Mind maps typically start with a central concept or question, with related ideas branching out. This method not only enhances creativity but also improves memory retention and helps generate new connections between ideas.
