What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EI) refers to the ability to recognize, understand, and manage our own emotions while also being aware of the emotions of others. Enhancing EI is essential for personal growth, effective communication, and building healthy relationships.

The Importance of Self-Reflection in Developing Emotional Intelligence

Self-reflection plays a crucial role in enhancing emotional intelligence. By taking time to reflect on our thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, we can gain insights into our emotional patterns and triggers. This awareness allows us to manage our responses and improve our interactions with others.
