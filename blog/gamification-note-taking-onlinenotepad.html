What is Gamification in Education?

Gamification refers to applying game-like elements—such as point scoring, leaderboards, and challenges—to non-game environments. In education, it’s an effective way to engage students, motivate learning, and increase retention of information. By incorporating gamified elements into tools like OnlineNotepad.lol, learners can find the act of taking notes and organizing study materials more interactive and rewarding.
